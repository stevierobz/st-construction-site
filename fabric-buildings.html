Why Fabric?

The Smart Alternative
To Traditional Construction

Faster, more cost-effective, and more versatile than wood or steel — without compromising on durability.

01
⚡

Faster Project Delivery

Fabric buildings go up in a fraction of the time of conventional construction. Most structures are complete in days or weeks — not months — minimizing operational downtime and getting you operational faster.

02
💰

Lower Cost Per Square Foot

Compared to conventional steel or wood-framed buildings, tension fabric structures offer significant savings on both materials and labour — while delivering a long-service-life structure that pays for itself.

03
☀️

Natural Diffused Light

The translucent fabric membrane diffuses natural daylight throughout the interior, dramatically reducing lighting requirements and operating costs — bright, open working environments from day one.

04
🔩

Engineered for Our Climate

Every structure is professionally engineered to meet Alberta, BC, and Saskatchewan snow, wind, and seismic loading codes. Built for decades of performance in Canada's most demanding environments.

05
🚚

Relocatable by Design

Unlike permanent construction, most fabric buildings can be disassembled and relocated when your operational needs change. Protect your investment even when your operation moves.

06
🔧

Low Maintenance Costs

Galvanized steel frames paired with UV-stabilized fabric covers minimize long-term maintenance. S&T offers ongoing service programs — the only contractor in Western Canada offering this.
